How to Vote?

Voting is as easy as a few clicks! Simply log in to the system, select your choices and submit your vote.

Is it Secure?

Absolutely! We employ advanced encryption and security protocols to ensure the safety and confidentiality of every vote cast.

Who Can Participate?

All registered students and staff of Haramaya University are eligible to participate in the e-voting process.

Can I Change My Vote?

Once you submit your vote, it's final. Make sure to review your choices before confirming your selection.

Need Assistance?

Our dedicated support team is available 24/7 to assist you with any queries or technical issues you may encounter during the voting process.
